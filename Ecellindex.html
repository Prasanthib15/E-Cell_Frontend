About E-Cell NITK

E-Cell NITK is an organization run by students of NITK to enhance tecnological skills and business acumen in students in order to convert their ideas to ventures.

About E-SUMMIT

E-Summit is the flagship event of Entrepreneurship cell at NITK. Envisioned to creare an entrepreneral spirit amongst the next generation of graduates; it is a multi-faceted event that encompasses entrepreneurship talks, business. workshops and panel discussions and a pitching event.
